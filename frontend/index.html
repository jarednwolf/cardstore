How It Works

1. Order Intake: Your e-commerce store sends order data to DeckStack via our secure API.
2. Processing: Our system automatically calculates shipping rates across multiple carriers and generates optimized shipping labels.
3. Carrier Integration: We connect with USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL APIs to get real-time rates and create shipping labels.
4. Delivery: You receive print-ready labels, tracking information, and detailed analytics to optimize your shipping operations.
