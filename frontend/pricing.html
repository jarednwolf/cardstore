Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the beta pricing?
Beta pricing gives you 50% off our regular rates for the first 6 months. You'll get all current features plus free access to inventory management, order processing, and Shopify integration as they're released.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You can explore all features and see how DeckStack fits your card store operations.
When will inventory management be available?
Inventory management is our top priority and will be available within 4 weeks. All current subscribers will get free access as soon as it's released.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and ACH bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
